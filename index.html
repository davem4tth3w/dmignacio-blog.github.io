DAY 2

Visited SBECC, PAGASA Weather Station, First Aviation Academy and Vessel Traffic Management System

❮ ❯

SBECC

We make a bus stop at Subic Bay Exhibition Center then explore inside the buiding. One of the most interesting things to see is its abstract paintings and some of the unique structures of the furnitures.

❮ ❯

PAGASA

At the Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration (PAGASA). We attend a short briefing on how they use their technologies to monitor the weather conditons.

❮ ❯

FAA

When it comes to aircraft, exploring and discovering FAA (First Aviation Academy) reveals the complexity and importance of the agency in maintaining the safety and efficiency of the aviation industry. They also show us how they train students using flight simulations.

❮ ❯

VESSEL TRAFFIC MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The last stop of our second day was to visit the Vessel Traffic Management System in the port of Subic Bay. We gain knowledge of how they enhance the safety and efficiency of maritime navigation and improve the overall management of vessel traffic in busy.

DAY 3

Visited QBO INNOVATION HUB and PACKETWORX INC.

❮ ❯

QBO INNOVATION HUB

QBO, much like the traditional bahay kubo that inspired its name, serves as a contemporary hub where Filipino startups and other ecosystem participants can collaborate and thrive. From here, they teach us some of the unique essence of teamwork and communication. "Fall in love with the problem, not the solution - QBO"

❮ ❯

PACKETWORX INC.

The first LoRaWAN connectivity provider for the Internet of Things in the Philippines is Packetworx. They introduce us to the background and accomplishments of their company and share their knowledge on how they specialize in designing and developing reliable and optimized devices.

DAY 4

Visited LIGHT RAIL TRANSIT AUTHORITY and NATIONAL MUSEUM OF THE PHILIPPINES

❮ ❯

LRTA

Light Rail Transit Authority commonly known as LRTA. We explore where they manage and maintain the LRT. It offered us a profound understanding of the intricacies and advancements in LRTA's infrastructure. The opportunity to step onto the platform without any fare allowed us to appreciate firsthand the accessibility and convenience that LRTA provides to commuters.

❮ ❯

NATIONAL MUSEUM OF THE PHILIPPINES

We are amazed by the fascinating works of the well-known local artist and the stories behind thier artworks.

DAY 5

Visted 98 LABS and HYTECH POWER INC.

❮ ❯

98 LABS

98 LABS, the company that shared their remarkable journey of establishments, also provides us with valuable insights into the world of technology. It was truly inspiring experience to meet someone who built the company from nothing into something significant.

❮ ❯

HYTECH POWER INC.

The last company we visited was the Hytech Power Inc., a company focuses on providing solutions to industrial companies and education, aiming to cultivate highly productive Filipino manpower capable of contributing to a prosperous Filipino society. We were amazed by their inventions, whoch allow us to advance more in technology.

Exploring Baguio City

❮ ❯

Baguio City

On the last day of our tour, we explored the vibrant city of Baguio. Our adventure began at the Strawberry Farm, where we had the chance to pick fresh strawberries and enjoy the scenic views of the lush fields. Next, we visited the Chinese Bell Church, an exquisite example of oriental architecture and a peaceful spot for reflection. Our journey continued to Mines View Park, where we were captivated by the panoramic views of the mountainous landscape. Finally, we visited the Philippine Military Academy, a prestigious institution with a rich history and beautiful grounds. Each stop offered a unique glimpse into the cultural and natural beauty of Baguio City
